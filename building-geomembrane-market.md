<p><strong>The Building Geomembrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Rough</li><li>Double Rough</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The building geomembrane market can be divided into two main types: single rough and double rough geomembranes. Single rough geomembranes feature a textured surface on one side, enhancing friction and stability, making them ideal for various applications like landfill liners and wastewater containment. In contrast, double rough geomembranes have textured surfaces on both sides, providing superior grip and enhanced performance in challenging environments. This type is often used in projects requiring increased stability, such as slopes and embankments.</p></p>
