<p><p>The Glass Fiber Reinforced Polymer (GFRP) market is segmented by resin types: Polyester, Vinyl Ester, Epoxy, and Polyurethane. Epoxy-based GFRP provides high strength and durability, commonly used in aerospace and high-performance composites. Polyurethane GFRP combines flexibility and impact resistance, suitable for automotive and industrial components.
