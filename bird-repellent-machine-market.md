<p><p>The bird repellent machine market comprises various types designed to deter birds effectively. Sonic bird repellers emit audible sounds that disrupt feeding and nesting behaviors. Ultrasonic bird repellers use high-frequency sounds, inaudible to humans but irritating to birds, making them vacate the area.
