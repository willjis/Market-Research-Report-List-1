<p><ul><li>Horizontal Ram Extruder</li><li>Vertical Ram Extruder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ram Extruder market includes two primary types: Horizontal and Vertical Ram Extruders. Horizontal Ram Extruders operate with the material fed from the side, allowing for better gravity-assisted flow and efficient processing of larger volumes. In contrast, Vertical Ram Extruders feed material from the top, ideal for producing denser materials and promoting uniform mixing. Both types are essential in various industries for shaping plastic, metal, and other materials, each offering unique advantages based on the application requirements.</p></p>
