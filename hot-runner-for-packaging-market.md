<p><ul><li>Open Gate</li><li>Valve Gate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot runner systems in the packaging market are crucial for improving efficiency and product quality in injection molding. The two main types are open gate and valve gate systems. Open gate systems allow material to flow freely through the mold, providing simplicity and lower cost, but may lead to gate marks. Conversely, valve gate systems employ precise control mechanisms that minimize material waste and improve aesthetics by reducing gate scars, making them ideal for high-quality packaging applications.</p></p>
