<p><p>The Medium Density Polyethylene (MDPE) resins market is primarily divided into two production processes: the Autoclave Process and the Tubular Process. The Autoclave Process involves using high pressure in a reactor to polymerize ethylene, producing resins with consistent density and molecular weight, ideal for applications requiring toughness. In contrast, the Tubular Process uses a tubular reactor for continuous flow polymerization, yielding a high output and efficient resin production, suitable for a wide range of applications, including packaging and piping.</p></p>
