<p><p>The automotive structural adhesives market is primarily divided into two key types: epoxy and polyurethane. Epoxy adhesives are known for their superior bond strength, durability, and resistance to environmental factors, making them ideal for high-performance applications in vehicle assembly. On the other hand, polyurethane adhesives offer flexibility and excellent impact resistance, making them suitable for areas requiring some movement or vibration dampening. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing vehicle integrity, reducing weight, and improving overall performance.</p></p>
