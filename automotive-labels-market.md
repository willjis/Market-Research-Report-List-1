<p><p>The automotive labels market comprises various types based on application methods. Pressure sensitive labels are adhesive-backed and can be easily applied without heat or moisture. Glue-applied labels require adhesive application during installation. Heat transfer labels utilize heat to fuse the design onto the surface. In-mold labels are integrated during the manufacturing process, becoming part of the final product. Other market types may include digital or eco-friendly labels that cater to specific needs in the automotive industry, ensuring durability and compliance.</p></p>
