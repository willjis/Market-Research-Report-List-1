<p><p>Electromagnetic shielding films for flexible printed circuits (FPC) can be categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Conductive Adhesive Type**: These films utilize conductive adhesives to bond shielding materials to surfaces, effectively blocking EMI while maintaining flexibility.</p><p>2. **Metal Alloy Type**: Composed of various metal alloys, these films provide robust shielding through their intrinsic conductive properties, ideal for high-frequency applications.</p><p>3. **Metal Microneedle Type**: Utilizing tiny metal needles, these films create a unique shielding mechanism that enhances performance by improving contact with surfaces, offering increased efficiency in EMI protection.</p></p>
