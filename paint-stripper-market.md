Acidic strippers rely on acids such as phosphoric acid, effective for removing rust and specific coatings. Other types include solvent-based or bio-based strippers, offering eco-friendly or specialized solutions. Each type caters to different surfaces, paint types, and environmental considerations, ensuring versatility in industrial, automotive, and DIY applications.</p></p>
