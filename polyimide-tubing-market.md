<p><ul><li>Seamless Tubing</li><li>Spiral Wound Tubing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyimide tubing market comprises two primary types: seamless tubing and spiral wound tubing. Seamless tubing is characterized by uniform wall thickness and excellent mechanical strength, making it ideal for applications that require high-temperature and chemical resistance. Conversely, spiral wound tubing features a helical design that offers flexibility and adaptability, making it suitable for applications involving bending or movement. Both types are widely used in aerospace, electronics, and medical industries due to their outstanding thermal stability and durability.</p></p>
