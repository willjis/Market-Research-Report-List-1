<p><p>The Laparoscopic Surgery Retrieval Pouch market is categorized into two main types: Manual Opening and Automatic Opening. Manual Opening pouches require surgeons to physically open the pouch to retrieve specimens, offering tactile control but potentially increasing handling time. In contrast, Automatic Opening pouches utilize mechanisms that allow for easier and quicker opening, streamlining the surgical process and enhancing efficiency. Both types aim to facilitate safe specimen retrieval while minimizing the risk of contamination during laparoscopic procedures.</p></p>
