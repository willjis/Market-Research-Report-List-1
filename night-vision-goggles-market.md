<p><strong>The Night-vision Goggles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The night-vision goggles market is primarily segmented into two types: image intensifier and thermal imaging. Image intensifier goggles amplify available light, allowing users to see in low-light conditions by enhancing existing illumination. In contrast, thermal imaging goggles detect heat emitted by objects, creating images based on temperature differences, making them effective in total darkness or obscured environments. Both technologies serve critical roles in military, law enforcement, and civilian applications, offering unique advantages for various visibility challenges during nighttime operations.</p></p>
