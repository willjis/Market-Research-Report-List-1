<p><p>Vacuum Jacketed Pipe (VJP) is an advanced thermal insulation solution primarily used for the transportation of cryogenic liquids, such as liquefied natural gas (LNG), hydrogen, and nitrogen. These pipes consist of an inner pipe surrounded by an outer pipe, with a vacuum created in the space between them to minimize thermal exchange and prevent heat ingress. This technology is essential for maintaining the low temperatures required in various industrial applications, reducing efficiency losses, and enhancing safety.</p><p>The Vacuum Jacketed Pipe Market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 5.9% during the forecast period.
