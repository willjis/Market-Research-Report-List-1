<p><ul><li>Wavefront Guided LASIK Surgery</li><li>Wavefront Optimized LASIK Surgery</li><li>Topography Guided LASIK Surgery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LASIK surgery market features three primary types: Wavefront Guided LASIK, which uses advanced measurements to tailor the procedure to the individual’s unique corneal map for greater precision; Wavefront Optimized LASIK, designed to correct refractive errors while preserving the natural shape of the cornea; and Topography Guided LASIK, which incorporates detailed imaging of the eye’s surface to address irregularities. Each type aims to enhance visual outcomes and improve patient satisfaction through customized correction approaches.</p></p>
