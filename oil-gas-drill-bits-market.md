<p><p>The Oil & Gas Drill Bits market primarily features two types: Roller Cone and Fixed Cutter bits. Roller Cone bits use rotating cones with sharp teeth that effectively crush rock formations, making them suitable for various drilling environments. In contrast, Fixed Cutter bits are characterized by their stationary cutting elements made from hard materials like diamond or carbide, designed for precise cutting through tough geological formations. Both types serve essential roles in optimizing drilling efficiency and effectiveness in the oil and gas sector.</p></p>
