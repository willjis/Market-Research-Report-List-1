<p><p>The Transfer Pipette Market is primarily divided into two types: single-channel and multichannel transfer pipettes. Single-channel transfer pipettes are designed for precise liquid handling in a one-at-a-time approach, making them ideal for applications requiring accuracy and attention to detail. In contrast, multichannel transfer pipettes allow simultaneous liquid transfer across multiple channels, enhancing efficiency and throughput in high-volume tasks. Both types serve essential roles in laboratories, hospitals, and research settings, catering to varied experimental needs and operational scales.</p></p>
