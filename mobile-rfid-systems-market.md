<p><p>Mobile RFID systems encompass various market types classified by frequency bands: LF (Low Frequency) RFID operates at 125-134 kHz, suitable for short-range applications like animal tracking; HF (High Frequency) RFID functions at 13.56 MHz, commonly used in access control and public transportation; UHF (Ultra High Frequency) RFID ranges from 300 MHz to 3 GHz, ideal for inventory management and supply chain applications due to its longer read distances; "Others" may include specialized systems like microwave RFID and emerging technologies.</p></p>
