<p><strong>The Hydraulic Turbine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impulse Hydraulic Turbine</li><li>Reaction Hydraulic Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic turbine market is primarily segmented into two types: impulse and reaction turbines. Impulse hydraulic turbines operate by converting the energy from a high-velocity water jet into mechanical energy, commonly seen in Pelton wheels. They are suited for high-head applications. In contrast, reaction hydraulic turbines generate power through the water's pressure and movement within the turbine, with Francis and Kaplan turbines being common examples. This type is ideal for lower heads and greater flow rates, catering to diverse energy generation needs.</p></p>
