<p><p>The 2D Covalent Organic Framework (COF) market is categorized into two main types based on pore sizes: 1.2 nm and 1.5-1.8 nm. The 1.2 nm COFs are typically utilized in applications requiring ultrafine filtration and gas storage due to their narrow pores, allowing selective molecular sieving. In contrast, the 1.5-1.8 nm COFs offer a balance of permeability and selectivity, making them suitable for applications in catalysis, sensors, and drug delivery, where larger molecules may need to be accommodated.</p></p>
