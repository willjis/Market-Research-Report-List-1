<p><p>The mineral wool insulation market comprises two primary types: glass wool and stone wool. Glass wool, made from recycled glass, offers excellent thermal and acoustic insulation, making it ideal for residential and commercial buildings. It is lightweight and resistant to moisture. Stone wool, derived from volcanic rock or basalt, provides superior fire resistance, soundproofing, and thermal insulation, making it suitable for industrial applications and high-temperature environments. Both types contribute to energy efficiency and sustainability in construction.</p></p>
