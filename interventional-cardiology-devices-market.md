<p><p>The interventional cardiology devices market comprises several key categories critical for treating cardiovascular diseases. Angioplasty balloons are used to expand narrowed arteries, while angioplasty stents provide structural support to keep vessels open. Catheters facilitate various diagnostic and therapeutic procedures. Plaque modification devices help remove or reduce atherosclerotic plaques, and hemodynamic flow alteration devices optimize blood flow dynamics. Together, these devices play a vital role in minimally invasive cardiac procedures, improving patient outcomes and enhancing the quality of care.</p></p>
