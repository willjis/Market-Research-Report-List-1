<p><p>The polymeric paver sand market comprises two primary types: polymeric paver sand and polymeric paver dust. Polymeric paver sand is designed to fill joints between pavers, providing stability and preventing weed growth while offering flexibility and durability. It activates when wet, creating a strong bond. On the other hand, polymeric paver dust is a finer material used for filling smaller gaps, enhancing the appearance and structural integrity of paver installations. Both products enhance outdoor spaces while ensuring longevity and maintenance ease.</p></p>
