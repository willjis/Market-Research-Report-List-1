<p><p>Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCBs) are essential safety devices used to protect electrical circuits from overload and short circuits. The market categorizes MCBs into three main types based on their tripping characteristics. Type B MCBs trip between 3 to 5 times the rated current, suitable for residential applications. Type C MCBs trip between 5 to 10 times the rated current, ideal for commercial settings with inductive loads. Type D MCBs, tripping at 10 to 20 times the rated current, are designed for heavy machinery and industrial applications.</p></p>
