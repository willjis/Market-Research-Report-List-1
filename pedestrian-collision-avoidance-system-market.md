<p><p>The **Pedestrian Collision Avoidance System (PCAS)** market includes various types based on detection technologies. **Video PCAS** uses cameras and image processing to identify pedestrians, offering high-resolution visuals but may struggle in low light. **Infrared PCAS** relies on thermal imaging, excelling in poor visibility conditions like fog or night. **Radar PCAS** uses radio waves for detection, providing reliable performance in diverse weather but with lower image detail. **Hybrid PCAS** combines multiple technologies (e.g., video, radar, infrared) for enhanced accuracy and adaptability across varying conditions.</p></p>
