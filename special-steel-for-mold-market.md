<p><p>The Special Steel for Mold market includes three main types: Hot Rolled Mold Steel, Cold Rolled Mold Steel, and Plastic Mold Steel. Hot Rolled Mold Steel is processed at high temperatures, providing improved durability and machinability, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Cold Rolled Mold Steel, produced at lower temperatures, offers enhanced dimensional accuracy and surface finish, suitable for precision molds. Plastic Mold Steel is specifically designed for manufacturing plastic molds, featuring optimal wear resistance and toughness to withstand the molding process.</p></p>
