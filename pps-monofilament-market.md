<p><p>The PPS (Polyphenylene Sulfide) monofilament market can be segmented into two main types: injection and extrusion. Injection molding involves injecting molten PPS into molds to produce specific shapes, ideal for precision components with complex designs. In contrast, extrusion involves forcing the PPS material through a die to create continuous shapes, such as filaments or rods, which can be cut to desired lengths. Both methods leverage the unique properties of PPS, including chemical resistance and thermal stability, to meet diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
