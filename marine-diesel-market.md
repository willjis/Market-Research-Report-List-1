<p><p>The marine diesel market comprises two primary types: Light Diesel Oil (LDO) and Heavy-Duty Diesel Oil (HDDO). LDO, characterized by its lower viscosity and ease of handling, is typically used in smaller vessels and for auxiliary engines due to its quick ignition properties. In contrast, HDDO is designed for larger, high-performance marine engines that require a more robust fuel capable of withstanding heavy operational conditions. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing fuel efficiency and engine performance in maritime operations.</p></p>
