<p><strong>The ANFO Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Type</li><li>Granular Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ANFO (Ammonium Nitrate Fuel Oil) market is categorized into two primary types: powder and granular. Powder-type ANFO is composed of finely granulated ammonium nitrate mixed with fuel oil, offering effective detonation in varied conditions, particularly in underground mining. In contrast, granular-type ANFO consists of coarser ammonium nitrate particles, providing improved flow characteristics and storage efficiency. Both types are utilized for their explosive properties in construction, mining, and quarrying, with preferences differing based on application requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
