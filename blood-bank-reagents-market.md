<p><p>The Blood Bank Reagents market comprises various types essential for blood screening and transfusion compatibility testing. Anti-Sera Reagents are used to detect specific blood group antigens, facilitating blood typing. Reagent Red Blood Cells serve as controls in hemagglutination tests for compatibility checking. Anti-Human Globulin reagents are crucial for identifying sensitized red blood cells during cross-matching. Blood Bank Saline acts as a diluent and is pivotal in preparing samples for testing. Collectively, these reagents ensure safe and effective blood transfusions.</p></p>
