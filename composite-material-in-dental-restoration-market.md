Poly-acid modified composites, also known as compomers, combine resin and glass ionomer properties, providing good aesthetics with enhanced fluoride release. Both types cater to diverse dental needs, emphasizing performance, longevity, and patient satisfaction in restorative dentistry.</p></p>
