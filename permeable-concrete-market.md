<p><strong>The Permeable Concrete Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pervious Concrete</li><li>Porous Asphalt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The permeable concrete market encompasses materials like pervious concrete and porous asphalt, designed to enhance water drainage and reduce surface runoff. Pervious concrete features a high void content, allowing water to flow through while supporting vehicle loads, making it ideal for driveways and parking areas. Porous asphalt, on the other hand, combines asphalt with voids to facilitate water infiltration, commonly used in roadways and parking lots. Both types promote sustainable urban drainage and mitigate flooding risks, supporting eco-friendly construction practices.</p></p>
