<p><ul><li>Touch Button Faucet</li><li>Infra-red Sensor Faucet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic induction tap market encompasses two primary types: touch button faucets and infra-red sensor faucets. Touch button faucets enable users to activate water flow with a simple push of a button, ensuring convenience and control. In contrast, infra-red sensor faucets employ motion detection technology to automatically dispense water when hands are detected nearby, promoting hygiene and reducing water waste. Both types aim to enhance user experience, improve sanitation, and provide efficient water management in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
