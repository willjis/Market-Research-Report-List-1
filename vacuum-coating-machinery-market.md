<p><p>The vacuum coating machinery market comprises various types, including vacuum evaporation coating machines, which deposit materials by heating them in a vacuum; vacuum sputtering coating machines, which utilize energetic particles to dislodge target materials onto substrates; and chemical vapor deposition (CVD) coating machines, which generate thin films through chemical reactions in a gaseous phase.
