Drill rods are long cylindrical tubes that connect the drill bit to the rig, facilitating torque transfer. Hammers, particularly down-the-hole hammers, enhance drilling efficiency by delivering powerful impacts to break rock. Additional accessories may include casings, swivel joints, and mud pumps, all contributing to the drilling process's efficiency and effectiveness.</p></p>
