<p><p>The In-Display Fingerprint Sensor market encompasses three main types: </p><p>1. **Optical In-Display Fingerprint Sensor** uses light to capture fingerprint images, providing a cost-effective solution with good accuracy.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Ultrasonic In-Display Fingerprint Sensor** employs ultrasound waves to create a 3D image of the fingerprint, offering higher security and performance in diverse conditions.</p><p>3. **Capacitive In-Display Fingerprint Sensor** detects the capacitance change when a finger touches the display, delivering fast and reliable authentication. Each type caters to varying security needs and technological requirements in consumer devices.</p></p>
