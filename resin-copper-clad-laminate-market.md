<p><p>Resin Copper Clad Laminate (CCL) is a key material used in the production of printed circuit boards (PCBs). It consists of a fibrous substrate, usually made of glass fiber or paper, impregnated with resin, and coated with a thin layer of copper on one or both sides. This combination of materials provides the electrical conductivity required for circuit designs while also offering mechanical stability and thermal resilience.</p><p>The Resin Copper Clad Laminate Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the rising demand for electronic devices across various sectors, including consumer electronics, automotive, telecommunications, and aerospace. BMI (Bismaleimide) is used in high-temperature, aerospace applications, while Others include specialized resins catering to niche requirements.</p></p>
