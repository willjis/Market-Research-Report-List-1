<p><p>The CNC slitting machine market features various types based on cutting mechanisms. Circular knife segmentation involves the use of rotating blades to slice materials with precision and smooth edges, ideal for flexible materials like plastics and textiles. Round blade segmentation utilizes a round cutting tool for straight-line cuts, perfect for thicker materials. Extrusion molding refers to the method of shaping materials by forcing them through a die, commonly used for continuous profiles, complementing slitting processes by providing uniform raw material dimensions.</p></p>
