<p><strong>The Micro Hydraulic Cylinder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Acting Hydraulic Cylinder</li><li>Double Acting Hydraulic Cylinder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro hydraulic cylinder market comprises two main types: single acting and double acting hydraulic cylinders. Single acting hydraulic cylinders utilize pressure to extend, using a spring or gravity for retraction; they are simple and cost-effective, making them ideal for light-duty applications. In contrast, double acting hydraulic cylinders use hydraulic pressure for both extension and retraction, providing more power and control, making them suitable for more demanding tasks in industries like robotics and automation. Both types enhance precision and efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
