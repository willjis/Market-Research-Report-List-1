<p><p>The medical suction regulators market comprises two primary types: continuous suction regulators and intermittent suction regulators. Continuous suction regulators provide a constant level of negative pressure, ideal for procedures requiring steady fluid removal, such as in surgeries or emergency care. In contrast, intermittent suction regulators deliver suction in cycles, allowing for controlled fluid management during procedures like wound care or respiratory therapy. Both types serve crucial roles in enhancing patient safety and comfort in various medical settings.</p></p>
