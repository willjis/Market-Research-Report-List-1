<p><p>The Automotive 4WD Parts Market includes key components like Two Differentials, Transfer Cases, and Others. Two Differentials distribute power between the front and rear axles, enabling smooth turns and traction. Transfer Cases split engine power between axles, allowing drivers to switch between 2WD and 4WD modes. Other parts include driveshafts, locking hubs, and control modules, which enhance performance and functionality. These components work together to optimize off-road capability, stability, and power distribution in 4WD vehicles.</p></p>
