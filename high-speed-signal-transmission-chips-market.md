<p><p>The high-speed signal transmission chips market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Relay Chips** facilitate the control of power paths in circuits, ensuring reliable signal integrity.  </p><p>2. **Switch Chips** manage data routing, enabling efficient communication between devices.   </p><p>3. **Distribute Chips** ensure signals are evenly distributed across multiple outputs, enhancing performance in complex systems.  </p><p>4. **Matrix Switch Chips** provide flexible routing of multiple input and output signals, allowing for dynamic signal management in applications like audio/video systems. Together, they support high-speed data transfer in various technologies.  </p></p>
