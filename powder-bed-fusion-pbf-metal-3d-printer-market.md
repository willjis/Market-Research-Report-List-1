<p><ul><li>Single Laser</li><li>Multiple Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Powder Bed Fusion (PBF) metal 3D printing can be categorized into two main types based on the number of lasers used: single laser and multiple laser systems. Single laser PBF systems utilize one focused laser to selectively fuse metal powder layer by layer, ideal for precision parts in lower production volumes. In contrast, multiple laser systems employ multiple lasers simultaneously, significantly increasing processing speed and efficiency, making them suitable for high-volume production with complex geometries. Both methods serve diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
