<p><p>Product customization software is offered in two main types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises software is installed and maintained on a company's own servers, providing full control over data and security but requiring significant upfront investment and IT resources. In contrast, cloud-based software is hosted on the vendor's servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs. It allows easy access from various devices and locations, facilitating collaboration and real-time updates for users in diverse environments.</p></p>
