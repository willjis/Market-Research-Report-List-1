<p><strong>The Electronic Flight Bag Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Portable EFB</li><li>Installed EFB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Flight Bag (EFB) market comprises two main types: Portable EFBs and Installed EFBs. Portable EFBs are lightweight, mobile devices that pilots can easily carry, enabling access to critical flight information and applications during pre-flight and in-flight operations. In contrast, Installed EFBs are integrated systems within the aircraft, offering a more permanent solution with enhanced capabilities and connectivity to the aircraft's avionics. Both types are designed to streamline operations, improve efficiency, and enhance safety in aviation.</p></p>
