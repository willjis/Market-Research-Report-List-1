<p><p>Self-aligning ball bearings are designed to accommodate misalignment between the shaft and the housing, enabling smooth operation even in challenging conditions. They consist of two rows of balls that are located in a spherical outer raceway, which allows for a certain degree of angular misalignment without affecting performance. This feature makes them ideal for applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, where shaft misalignment can occur due to fluctuating loads and vibrations.</p><p>The Self-aligning Ball Bearing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9% during the forecast period. Additionally, advancements in materials and manufacturing techniques are enhancing the durability and efficiency of these bearings. Recent trends indicate a shift towards the use of self-aligning ball bearings in electric vehicles and renewable energy applications, capitalizing on their ability to adapt to varying operational conditions.
