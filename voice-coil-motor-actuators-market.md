<p><ul><li>Moving Coil Type Voice Coil Motor Actuators</li><li>Moving Magnet Type Voice Coil Motor Actuators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Voice Coil Motor Actuators market is primarily categorized into two types: Moving Coil Type and Moving Magnet Type. </p><p>Moving Coil Type Voice Coil Motors consist of a coil suspended in a magnetic field, where the electrical current generates motion. This design allows for high precision and rapid response. </p><p>In contrast, Moving Magnet Type Voice Coil Motors feature magnets attached to the moving component, with the coil fixed in place. This setup provides greater force efficiency and is often used in applications requiring robust performance and durability.</p></p>
