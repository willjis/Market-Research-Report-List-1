<p><strong>The OLED Organic Terminal Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EML</li><li>HTL</li><li>ETL</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED organic terminal materials market is divided into four main types: Emissive Layer (EML), Hole Transport Layer (HTL), Electron Transport Layer (ETL), and others. EML comprises materials that emit light when excited, playing a crucial role in color production. HTL facilitates the movement of holes from the anode, enhancing efficiency, while ETL allows electrons to travel from the cathode. Other materials may include substrates or encapsulants that support the OLED structure and enhance performance.</p></p>
