<p><ul><li>Soft-coat Glass</li><li>Hard-coat Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat reflective glass market features two primary types: soft-coat glass and hard-coat glass. Soft-coat glass is manufactured by depositing a thin layer of metallic material, offering superior thermal insulation and enhanced solar control. It is generally used in commercial applications and high-performance buildings. In contrast, hard-coat glass has a durable, baked-on coating that provides basic heat reflection and is less sensitive to scratches. This type is often employed in residential buildings for its cost-effectiveness and ease of maintenance.</p></p>
