<p><p>The inhalation anesthetic market comprises several key types, including Sevoflurane, Isoflurane, Desflurane, and others. Sevoflurane is favored for its rapid onset and recovery times, making it suitable for outpatient procedures. Isoflurane is noted for its potency and cardiovascular stability but has a slower recovery phase. Desflurane, known for its low solubility, allows for quick changes in anesthesia depth, though it's more expensive. The "Others" category includes less common agents and emerging inhalation anesthetics, serving diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
