<p><p>Topping dispenser systems are categorized into manual and automatic types. Manual dispensers require users to operate them, allowing for controlled dispensing but often resulting in variability in portion sizes. They are typically cost-effective and simple to maintain. In contrast, automatic dispensers utilize sensors or timers for precise and consistent dispensing, improving efficiency and reducing waste. These systems are often integrated with modern technology, offering features like programmable settings and touchless operation, making them ideal for high-traffic environments.</p></p>
