<p><strong>The Consumer Telematics Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrated Telematics</li><li>Embedded Telematics</li><li>Tethered Telematics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Consumer telematics systems encompass three main market types: integrated, embedded, and tethered telematics. Integrated telematics combine hardware and software within the vehicle, offering seamless functionality and real-time data processing. Embedded telematics involve built-in systems that operate independently of external devices, providing features like navigation and diagnostics. Tethered telematics rely on smartphone connectivity to access services and data, enhancing user experience through mobile apps. Each type caters to different consumer needs, emphasizing convenience, connectivity, and enhanced vehicle performance.</p></p>
