<p><p>CNC lathes and turning centers are essential tools in precision machining, with market types categorized as vertical and horizontal. Vertical CNC lathes are often used for larger, heavier workpieces, allowing efficient gravity-assisted chip removal, and are ideal for large-scale production. In contrast, horizontal CNC turning centers cater to smaller, intricate parts, offering enhanced accessibility and faster loading/unloading processes. Each type serves distinct applications, influencing design choices based on factors like space, production volume, and component complexity.</p></p>
