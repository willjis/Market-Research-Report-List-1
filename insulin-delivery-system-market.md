<p><p>The insulin delivery system market encompasses three primary types: insulin syringes, insulin pumps, and insulin pens. Insulin syringes are traditional devices that provide a straightforward method for administering insulin, typically used for precise dosing. Insulin pumps deliver continuous insulin via a programmable device, allowing for better blood glucose control and lifestyle convenience. Insulin pens are portable, user-friendly devices that combine the functionality of syringes with a pre-filled insulin cartridge, making them suitable for daily management of diabetes while enhancing patient compliance.</p></p>
