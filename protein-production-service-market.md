<p><p>The Protein Production Service Market encompasses various expression systems used to produce proteins for research and therapeutic purposes. Prokaryotic expression systems, such as E. coli, are cost-effective and rapid but may lack post-translational modifications. Mammalian cell expression systems, like CHO cells, provide authentic folding and modifications, making them suitable for complex proteins. Yeast cell expression systems, such as Saccharomyces cerevisiae, offer a balance of cost and post-translational modifications. Other systems include insect cells and plant-based expression, catering to specific needs in protein development.</p></p>
