<p><strong>The Intracranial Pressure Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Invasive</li><li>Non-invasive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intracranial pressure (ICP) monitor market is segmented into invasive and non-invasive types. Invasive monitors involve the placement of devices, such as intracranial pressure catheters, directly into the cranial cavity, providing accurate and continuous readings of pressure. Non-invasive methods, like transcranial Doppler ultrasound or optical techniques, measure ICP without penetrating the skull, offering safer alternatives with fewer complications. The choice between invasive and non-invasive monitoring depends on clinical requirements, patient conditions, and the need for precision in pressure assessment.</p></p>
