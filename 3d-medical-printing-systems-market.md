<p><p>The 3D Medical Printing Systems market encompasses various technologies, including: </p><p>1. **Stereolithography (SLA)**, which uses a UV laser to solidify liquid resin, allowing for high-precision models.</p><p>2. **Digital Light Processing (DLP)**, similar to SLA but employs digital light to cure resin more quickly.</p><p>3. **Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM)**, which extrudes thermoplastic material layer by layer for creating robust parts.</p><p>4. **Selective Laser Sintering (SLS)**, utilizing a laser to fuse powdered materials, ideal for complex geometries.</p><p>5. **Electronic Beam Melting (EBM)**, featuring a focused electron beam to melt metal powder for high-strength implants.</p><p>6. **Others**, encompassing emerging techniques and hybrid methods.</p></p>
