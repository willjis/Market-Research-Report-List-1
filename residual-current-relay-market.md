<p><p>The Residual Current Relay (RCR) market can be categorized into two main types: DIN rail and panel-mounted configurations. DIN rail RCRs are designed for installation on standard DIN rails within electrical distribution boards, promoting ease of setup and space-saving. In contrast, panel-mounted RCRs are installed directly onto control panels or enclosures, offering flexibility in various applications. Both types serve the critical function of detecting ground faults and protecting electrical circuits, enhancing safety in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
