<p><strong>The Automotive Active Roll Control System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Active Roll Control (EARC)</li><li>Hydraulic Active Roll Control (HARC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Active Roll Control System market comprises two primary types: Electric Active Roll Control (EARC) and Hydraulic Active Roll Control (HARC). EARC utilizes electric actuators to adjust the vehicle's suspension dynamics, enhancing stability and comfort by reducing body roll during cornering. In contrast, HARC employs hydraulic actuators to manage roll resistance, providing a robust response for heavy-duty applications. Both systems aim to improve vehicle handling, safety, and passenger comfort by dynamically controlling the vehicle's body posture in various driving conditions.</p></p>
