<p><ul><li>Reserve Price</li><li>No Reserve Price</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In online auctions, the market types can be classified into two main categories: reserve price and no reserve price. A reserve price auction has a minimum price set by the seller, ensuring the item won't be sold unless bids reach that threshold. Conversely, a no reserve price auction allows the item to be sold to the highest bidder, regardless of the bid amount, potentially resulting in lower final sale prices but encouraging more bidding activity.</p></p>
