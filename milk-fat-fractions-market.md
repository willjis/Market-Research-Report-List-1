<p><p>The milk fat fractions market is segmented into low, medium, and high melting fractions. Medium melting fractions find applications in chocolates and bakery products, offering a balance between stability and creaminess. High melting fractions, often used in confectionery and frying fats, maintain structure at higher temperatures, providing desired texture and flavor stability in various food products.</p></p>
