<p><p>The Self-Monitoring Blood Glucose (SMBG) market includes several key components: glucose meters, test strips, and lancets. Glucose meters are electronic devices that measure blood sugar levels, providing users with immediate results. Test strips, which are used alongside glucose meters, contain reagents that react to glucose in a blood sample. Lancets are small, sharp devices used to prick the skin to obtain the blood sample. Together, these products enable individuals, especially those with diabetes, to effectively manage their blood glucose levels.</p></p>
