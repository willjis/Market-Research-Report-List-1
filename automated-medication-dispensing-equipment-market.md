<p><p>Automated Medication Dispensing Equipment (AMDE) consists of Centralized and Decentralized Dispensing Equipment. Centralized Dispensing Equipment is typically located in a central pharmacy area, allowing for bulk medication preparation and efficient inventory management, often benefiting larger healthcare facilities. In contrast, Decentralized Dispensing Equipment is positioned closer to patient care areas, such as nursing units, facilitating quicker access to medications and promoting timely administration. Both types enhance medication safety, streamline workflows, and improve overall patient care by minimizing manual handling.</p></p>
