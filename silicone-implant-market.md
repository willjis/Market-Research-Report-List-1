<p><p>The silicone implant market includes two primary types: silicone-filled and saline-filled implants. Silicone-filled implants use a cohesive silicone gel that mimics natural breast tissue, offering a softer, more natural feel. They are popular for cosmetic and reconstructive surgeries. Saline-filled implants contain sterile saltwater, providing a firmer feel and adjustable volume during surgery. Both types have silicone outer shells, but silicone implants are preferred for their natural aesthetics, while saline implants are often chosen for their safety and lower cost.</p></p>
