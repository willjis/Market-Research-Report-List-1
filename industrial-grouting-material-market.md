Epoxy-based grouts offer high strength, chemical resistance, and durability, making them ideal for heavy-load environments. The "others" category includes diverse formulations such as cementitious and acrylic grouts, used for specialized applications. Together, these materials address various needs in construction, repair, and infrastructure projects, ensuring structural integrity and longevity.</p></p>
