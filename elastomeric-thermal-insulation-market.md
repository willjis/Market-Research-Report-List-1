<p><p>Elastomeric thermal insulation materials are flexible, rubber-like foams used to prevent heat transfer in HVAC, refrigeration, and industrial systems. **NBR-based** insulation, derived from nitrile rubber, offers excellent resistance to oils and fuels, making it ideal for industrial applications. **EPDM-based** insulation, made from ethylene propylene diene monomer, excels in outdoor use due to its UV and weather resistance. **Chloroprene-based** insulation, known for its fire retardancy and durability, is commonly used in fire-prone environments. **Others** include blends or specialty materials tailored for unique performance requirements.</p></p>
