<p><strong>The Carbon Fiber Wraps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rayon Based</li><li>Pitch Based</li><li>Pan Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon fiber wraps market can be categorized into three key types: rayon-based, pitch-based, and PAN-based. Rayon-based wraps utilize regenerated cellulose fibers, offering good strength and flexibility but lower thermal resistance. Pitch-based wraps derive from petroleum pitch, providing high thermal stability and lower cost, making them ideal for specific applications. PAN-based wraps, produced from polyacrylonitrile, are known for their superior mechanical properties and are widely used in high-performance applications due to their strength, stiffness, and durability.</p></p>
