In contrast, deep cleaning involves a more comprehensive approach, including scrubbing grout, disinfecting all surfaces, and addressing hard-to-reach areas. This type is ideal for thorough sanitation and long-term cleanliness, catering to different customer needs and preferences.</p></p>
