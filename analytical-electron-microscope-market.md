<p><strong>The Analytical Electron Microscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmission Electron Microscope</li><li>Scanning Electron Microscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The analytical electron microscope market comprises primarily two types: Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEM) and Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEM). TEM provides high-resolution imaging by transmitting electrons through a thin sample, allowing for detailed internal structures. In contrast, SEM scans the surface of a sample with focused electrons, producing 3D images and surface morphology information. Both types are crucial in various fields, including materials science, biology, and nanotechnology, enabling advanced research and development through precise analysis at the micro and nanoscale.</p></p>
