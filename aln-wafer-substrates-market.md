<p><p>AlN wafer substrates are categorized into various types based on their specifications and applications. AN-170 typically features a thickness of 170 micrometers and is used for high-frequency devices due to its excellent thermal conductivity. AN-200, with a thicker profile of 200 micrometers, offers better mechanical stability and supports high-power applications. AN-230, at 230 micrometers, is designed for advanced optoelectronic devices, providing enhanced performance in light emission and absorption. Each type addresses specific industry needs, particularly in electronics and photonics.</p></p>
