<p><p>The fully automatic urine analyzer market is primarily categorized into two methods: microscopy and chemistry. The microscopy method utilizes advanced imaging techniques to analyze morphological features of cells and casts in urine, offering detailed insights for diagnosing various conditions. Conversely, the chemistry method focuses on assessing chemical properties and concentrations of substances in urine, such as glucose, protein, and pH levels, providing rapid and quantitative results for metabolic and systemic disorders. Both methods enhance diagnostic accuracy and efficiency in clinical laboratories.</p></p>
