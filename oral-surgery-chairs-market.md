<p><p>The oral surgery chairs market is divided into two main types: electric and manual chairs. In contrast, manual chairs rely on mechanical adjustments, requiring physical effort to reposition them. While electric chairs are often preferred for their ease of use and time efficiency, manual chairs can be more cost-effective and simpler to maintain, appealing to different segments of the market.</p></p>
