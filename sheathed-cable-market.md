<p><ul><li>Nonmetallic-Sheathed</li><li>Metallic-Sheathed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sheathed cable market is primarily divided into two main types: nonmetallic-sheathed and metallic-sheathed cables. Nonmetallic-sheathed cables, like NM (nobody duct) cables, are insulated with materials such as PVC and are widely used for residential wiring due to their flexibility and ease of installation. In contrast, metallic-sheathed cables, including armored and metal-clad cables, offer enhanced protection against physical damage and environmental factors, making them suitable for industrial and commercial applications. Both types cater to varying needs within electrical installations.</p></p>
