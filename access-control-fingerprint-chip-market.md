Capacitive Fingerprint Modules capture unique fingerprint patterns by using electrical currents, providing high accuracy and durability. In contrast, Optical Fingerprint Modules rely on light to capture detailed images of fingerprints, offering excellent image quality and ease of integration. Both types serve critical roles in enhancing security systems, catering to diverse applications across industries requiring reliable biometric authentication.</p></p>
