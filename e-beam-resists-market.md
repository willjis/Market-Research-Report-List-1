<p><strong>The E-beam Resists Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive E-beam Resists</li><li>Negative E-beam Resists</li></ul></p>
<p><p>E-beam resists are crucial materials used in electron beam lithography for creating precise patterns on substrates. The market is segmented into positive and negative e-beam resists. Positive e-beam resists become soluble after exposure to electron beams, allowing for finer patterning and higher resolution in applications like semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, negative e-beam resists remain insoluble when exposed, resulting in a more robust and durable pattern suitable for various microfabrication techniques. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance requirements.</p></p>
