<p><p>The Illuminated Tactile Switch market consists of two primary types: surface-mounting and through-hole mounting. Surface-mounting switches are designed for placement directly onto the surface of circuit boards, offering compact design and efficient space utilization, ideal for modern electronics. Conversely, through-hole mounting switches penetrate through the board, providing robust connections and better durability, making them suitable for larger devices or applications requiring greater mechanical strength. Both types cater to diverse industry needs, from consumer electronics to automotive applications.</p></p>
