<p><strong>The Food Grade Silica Sol Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Precipitated Silica</li><li>Fumed Silica</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food grade silica sol market is primarily divided into two types: precipitated silica and fumed silica. Precipitated silica is produced through a chemical reaction, resulting in a granular structure, making it ideal for applications like anti-caking agents and flow aids in food products. Fumed silica, on the other hand, is created through the combustion of silane, producing a highly porous structure that enhances viscosity and texture. Both types are utilized for their stabilizing and anti-clumping properties, crucial in food processing.</p></p>
