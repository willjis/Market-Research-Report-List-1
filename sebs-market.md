<p><strong>The SEBS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear SEBS</li><li>Radial SEBS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Linear SEBS and Radial SEBS are types of styrene-ethylene-butylene-styrene copolymers used in various industries. Linear SEBS has a straight-chain molecular structure, offering flexibility, durability, and excellent processability, making it ideal for adhesives, coatings, and soft-touch applications. Radial SEBS, with a branched molecular structure, provides enhanced elasticity, tensile strength, and thermal stability, making it suitable for high-performance applications like automotive parts and industrial goods. Both types cater to diverse market needs, balancing mechanical properties and processing ease for specific end-use requirements.</p></p>
