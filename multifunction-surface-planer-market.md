<p><p>The multifunction surface planer market comprises two primary types: vertical surface planers and horizontal surface planers. Vertical surface planers are designed for efficient vertical milling, allowing for precise leveling and finishing of materials, making them ideal for intricate, detailed work. In contrast, horizontal surface planers excel in handling larger workpieces and delivering smooth, flat surfaces quickly. Both types cater to diverse applications in woodworking, metalworking, and manufacturing, providing flexibility and precision for various production needs.</p></p>
